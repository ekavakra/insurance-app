How It Works

Our app makes it simple for users to receive financial support from their community during tough times. Follow the steps below to see how it works.

1. Register

Create an account and set up your nominee details in a few easy steps.

2. Nominate

Add a nominee who will receive the support on your behalf in case of a tragedy.

3. Request Support

Your nominee can request support, which is reviewed by our team and shared with the community.

4. Approve

Our team verifies and approves the support request to notify the community.

5. Donate

Community members contribute, and your nominee receives the financial support.

6. Complete

Once donations are complete, the support is transferred to your nominee’s account.
