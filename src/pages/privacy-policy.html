Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

We collect personal information that you provide when you sign up, such as your name, email address, and payment information. We may also collect data automatically, such as your IP address and browser type, through the use of cookies and other tracking technologies.

Use of Information

The information we collect is used to provide and improve our services. We use your data to process transactions, send notifications, and improve user experience. We may also use your information for marketing purposes if you have opted in to receive such communications.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to provide our services, comply with the law, or protect our rights. Any third-party service providers we work with are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information. This includes encryption, firewalls, and secure server environments to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information at any time. You can update your account information through the settings page or contact our support team to request changes or deletions.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant updates by email or through the app. Your continued use of our services after any updates constitutes acceptance of the changes.
